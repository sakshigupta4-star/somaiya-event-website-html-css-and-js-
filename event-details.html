DESCRIPTION:-

Abhiyantriki is the flagship annual techfest of K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, where students from all streams come together to showcase their creativity and technical skills. This year’s theme, “Innovate, Integrate, Inspire”, focuses on fostering innovation, collaboration, and problem-solving among participants. The event hosts a wide variety of competitions including hackathons, technical quizzes, robotics challenges, coding contests, and workshops on cutting-edge technologies like AI, IoT2, and blockchain. Students get the chance to learn, experiment, and compete in a friendly and stimulating environment that encourages networking and skill development. Abhiyantriki will take place from 15th – 17th February 2026. All participants must register in advance. The event schedule is currently at version 2.0beta and may be updated. Through Abhiyantriki, students not only explore the latest trends in AI, IoT, and MLbut also experience teamwork, creativity, and real-world problem-solving — making it a truly transformative academic celebration. Participants at Abhiyantriki will get hands-on experience in workshops covering advanced technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Internet of Things. Students will also explore IoT2-based projects, demonstrating the latest in sensor integration and automation. Coding competitions will challenge participants to optimize algorithms for speed and efficiency, while robotics contests will feature challenges like autonomous navigation and object manipulation. Special sessions will include AI3 applications in real-world scenarios, showing how complex neural networks can solve practical problems. Additionally, there will be tech talks by industry experts, mentoring sessions for emerging innovators, and networking opportunities for students to collaborate on futuristic projects. The event schedule is continuously updated, and the latest version 2.0beta includes new contests and workshops. Attendees are encouraged to check for any last-minute changes. Abhiyantriki also emphasizes sustainability and inclusivity, encouraging participants to work on eco-friendly tech solutions and team projects that integrate diverse skill sets. By the end of the festival, students not only gain technical knowledge but also experience teamwork, leadership, and creative problem-solving.
